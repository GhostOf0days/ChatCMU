Alumni News Update 09.20-09.26.22
Posted on September 19, 2022
Welcome to the weekly Alumni News Update! To have your news featured, please email schoolofartnews@cmu.edu with a brief description, link to more information, image, and your degree and class year. Or, add us to your newsletter list.
Cassandra C. Jones MFA ’04 has a solo online exhibition titled “Lighting Drawings” on Artsy organized by Mark Moore Fine Art, through Nov 20. 
Caroline Landau BFA ’13 exhibits in “Fired Up: Glass Today” at the Wadsworth Atheneum Museum of Art in Hartford, CT, through Feb 5. 
Several of Barbara Rosenthal’s BFA ’70 works were acquired by the Birth Rites Collection at the Gulbenkian Arts Centre, University of Kent in Canterbury, UK.
Michael Royce BFA ’11 had a two-person exhibition with Patrick Gantert titled “Hamster Wheel” at Headstone Gallery in Kingston, NY. 
X-Tra Magazine published a conversation between Carrie Schneider BHA ’01 and Kaja Silverman in their most recent issue. 
Fereshteh Toosi MFA ’04 was included in Issue 58 of Antennae: The Journal of Nature in Visual Culture. 
Mary Tremonte BHA ’00 exhibits in “The Pittsburgh Left” at Space Gallery, through Oct 23. 
Ryan Woodring BFA ’10 has a solo exhibition at the International Museum of Surgical Science titled “Today and Possibly Tomorrow” on view through Nov 13.
