 CMU Travel Information
This site shares tools, processes and resources on the things you need to know before, during and after traveling for CMU business. The university has negotiated rates, discounts and arrangements for a variety of your travel needs and is here to offer you the support you need to have a smooth travel experience.
Collegiate Travel Planners (CTP), powered by Corporate Travel Management (CTM),  is the university’s single preferred travel supplier. CTP provides fulfillment via Concur Travel. CMU travelers and travel arrangers are strongly encouraged to utilize CTP as a comprehensive "one-stop-shop" to leverage a variety of traveler and university benefits.
