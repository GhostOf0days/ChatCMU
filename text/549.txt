Facilities Management Services
                                      ›              Services
                            


 Our teams provide a variety of services to the CMU community, including the areas listed below, with the help of designated campus-wide Facility Coordinators. Many of our services are provided at no charge. Learn more about who pays for repairs to see which costs are covered by Facilities Management Services and which are covered by the customer. You can also view our rates or request a cost estimate for your project.
Please contact us for more information about the following services:

Academic and research support
Access control and keys (including bollard gate access)
Capital renewal management
Custodial services
Disaster remediation and restoration 
Event support
Green practices and sustainability services
Grounds and landscaping
Life safety systems
Maintenance and repair, including preventative maintenance
Materials for order via the FMS Storeroom
Painting (view our standard paint colors [pdf] and paint colors for specific campus buildings [pdf])
Renewal and minor renovation 
Solid waste management
Utility operations and systems administration

 
Submit, or check the status of, a service request, or order materials from our FMS Storeroom.


Work with us! Vendors interested in serving our facilities teams should review our supplier information. Want to join our team? Learn more about our career opportunities.

  
 Facility Coordinators
Carnegie Mellon has designated Facility Coordinators across campus to help manage facilities needs and information for their respective areas. View the Facility Coordinator list to find the contact(s) for your department. Your coordinator can help process non-urgent facilities requests, including event support, and alert you to important facilities updates and alerts.
 
View list of Facility Coordinators by area

 

 Need help? Contact our Service Response Center, your area’s Facility Coordinator or one of our maintenance zone managers.



FMS Team Resources


                                             
                                    

Facilities Management Services is part of CMU’s Facilities, Infrastructure and Risk Management (FIRM) group.

                                      ›              Services
                            


 Our teams provide a variety of services to the CMU community, including the areas listed below, with the help of designated campus-wide Facility Coordinators. Many of our services are provided at no charge. Learn more about who pays for repairs to see which costs are covered by Facilities Management Services and which are covered by the customer. You can also view our rates or request a cost estimate for your project.
Please contact us for more information about the following services:

Academic and research support
Access control and keys (including bollard gate access)
Capital renewal management
Custodial services
Disaster remediation and restoration 
Event support
Green practices and sustainability services
Grounds and landscaping
Life safety systems
Maintenance and repair, including preventative maintenance
Materials for order via the FMS Storeroom
Painting (view our standard paint colors [pdf] and paint colors for specific campus buildings [pdf])
Renewal and minor renovation 
Solid waste management
Utility operations and systems administration

Work with us! Vendors interested in serving our facilities teams should review our supplier information. Want to join our team? Learn more about our career opportunities.

  
 Facility Coordinators
Carnegie Mellon has designated Facility Coordinators across campus to help manage facilities needs and information for their respective areas. View the Facility Coordinator list to find the contact(s) for your department. Your coordinator can help process non-urgent facilities requests, including event support, and alert you to important facilities updates and alerts.
 
View list of Facility Coordinators by area

 

 Need help? Contact our Service Response Center, your area’s Facility Coordinator or one of our maintenance zone managers.
