Welcome to SCS / ECE Careers!Recruiters are encouraged to connect with our students through SCS / ECE Let's Talk!, campus career fairs, information sessions, technical presentations, course sponsorships, the SCS / ECE Professional Series, the Ph.D. Seminar, meet-and-greet programs, resume collections, and involvement in an array of unique student and division programming. This year, because of the changing circumstances, we are embracing the power of virtual. See our scheduled programs.Remember, we always welcome your ideas and feedback!To recruit students at the Carnegie Mellon School of Computer Science and Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering, please REGISTER with SCS / ECE CareersWe also welcome an opportunity to talk with you directly — 412-268-8525 | careers@cs.cmu.edu | ece-careers@cmu.edu— to better assess your needs, to explore opportunities and worthwhile programs for interacting with all our students, while exploring our academic programs and scientific/research parallels.SCS/ECE Careers connects students with employers through career fairs, conferences and special events — offering the opportunity to showcase their research and educational accomplishments. View current events and deadlines on our site.A variety of jobs within CMU are available to motivated students to provide professional experience and advancement, while affording additional financial resources.Students should search for work-study and federal community service positions using Handshake.
