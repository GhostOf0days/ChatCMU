 Welcome Home, Tartans!
Whether you graduated last year or 50 years ago, Carnegie Mellon is your home.
And this website is your connection to CMU, your fellow alumni, faculty and staff, and current students. Explore all of the activities, events and resources available for you to deepen your experience as an alum.
Don't hesitate to reach out to the CMU Alumni Association staff if you need additional information or assistance. And check back frequently — exciting events and news are on the way!
 



Contact Us
Email the Alumni Association or call us at 412-268-8089 to learn more about connecting with CMU and the global Tartan network.
