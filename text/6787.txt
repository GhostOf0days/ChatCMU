Open Figure Drawing

September 10 @ 6:00 pm - December 3 @ 8:30 pm 





« TQ Live! 2023
Mindy Seu Presents the Cyberfeminist Index »










Open Figure Drawing is free and open to the public. Held on Sunday afternoons, Open Figure is an opportunity to draw from live models in any medium. Open Figure provides a variety of models that range in sex, age, and ethnicities from which artists can work.
All sessions take place from 6:00 to 8:30 PM in the College of Fine Arts room 308.
