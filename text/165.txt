A Monumental Impact

We are grateful to those who are joining with us to turn bold ideas into meaningful progress, to anticipate and solve the world’s most pressing challenges, and to write the story of this century. Thank you!










							Our more than  56,732 supporters have made possible:						







50Endowed Professorships

Recruiting and empowering the scholars and educators who will revolutionize their fields — and our future.








180Endowed Scholarships

Creating access to a life-changing CMU education for students regardless of financial circumstances.








141Endowed Fellowships

Supporting the next generation of researchers and scholars — and preparing them to be the leaders the world needs.

Make Possible: The Campaign for Carnegie Mellon University
 is making a difference on our campus, in our community and around the world:














Advancing Student Health & Wellness


CMU is committed to giving students the multidimensional support they need to achieve. Key to those efforts is the CMU’s first-ever dedicated health, wellness and athletics facility, the Highmark Center for Health, Wellness and Athletics, made possible by a lead gift from Highmark Health.

Learn More













Reinventing Learning and Research Facilities


CMU is creating a physical environment that encourages collaboration across disciplines and learning without boundaries. Leading-edge facilities such as the Tepper Quad, ANSYS Hall, TCS Hall, Scaife Hall, and the upcoming science futures building and Robotics Innovation Center will be where the future is written.

Learn More













Developing New Fields of Discovery


CMU is leading the way at the intersection of technology and humanity, developing new centers and institutes that are working on the answers to the world’s most complex problems through interdisciplinary, collaborative research.

Learn More
