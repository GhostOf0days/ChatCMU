 Spring Carnival WeekendApril 11-14, 2024
For more than 100 years, Carnival has been — and continues to be — one the most anticipated weekends of the year with Tartans around the world coming together to celebrate this oh-so-CMU tradition. 
In addition to our traditions of Booth, Buggy, Kiltie Band concert, Scotch'n'Soda performances and Activities Board events, below are a few weekend highlights:

Reunion exclusive activities
Rides, games and inflatables
Mobot races
Midway Activities and Wellness tents with numerous activities and demos
All-Campus Community Lunch
College and departmental gatherings
Alumni Interest Network events
Food Truck Fest
Plus, the Carnival souvenir and so much more

 
Are you an alumnus from an undergraduate class ending in 4 and 9 (i.e. 1974 or 1999) or 2023?
If so, congratulations – we are celebrating YOU during Reunion Weekend at Carnival 2024. Learn more on the Reunions website.


Questions? Email cmuevents@andrew.cmu.edu.
Spring Carnival Chairs:
Angelica Gonzalez (TPR 2025) and Avineet Nanjappa (ENG 2025) Sweepstakes Chair: Ale Bonacini (ENG 2025)


We anticipate launching the full schedule and registration in February of 2024, so stay tuned!
