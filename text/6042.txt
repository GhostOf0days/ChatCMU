Inbar Hagai MFA ’24 Selected for Loop Art Critique Residency
Posted on August 30, 2023
MFA Candidate Inbar Hagai was selected for and participated in the fifth cohort of the Loop Art Critique residency. Loop Art Critique (LAC) is a gesamtkunstwerk, a comprehensive artwork comprising multiple art forms, situated within four distinct spaces. As an art space, LAC is committed to group critique and experimental exhibitions. It is located in Onland, a metaverse developed by art and technology non-profit, The MUD Foundation. The fifth cohort was selected by Lorna Mills.
Works of the participating artists are currently displayed as part of the group exhibition “Visual Verses” at The Salon through September 30. For a better viewing experience use of personal computer or VR headset recommended.
