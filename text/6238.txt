Professor Hubbard Exhibits in “Part One” at Company Gallery
Posted on December 12, 2022
Professor Katherine Hubbard‘s work is included in the exhibition “Part One,” on view at Company Gallery in New York City from December 8, 2022 through January 28, 2023. Drawing on the seminal body printing techniques devised by David Hammons, Hubbard will exhibit her experimental body prints made in the black and white darkroom. The exhibition also includes the work of TM Davy, Sylvie Fleury, Nash Glynn, Aurel Haize Odogbo, Sixten Sandra Österberg, Berenice Olmedo, and Joanne Robertson.
