Join us for the 2023 Diploma Ceremony
Posted on May 5, 2023
Please join us in celebrating the graduating class of 2023 at the School of Art Diploma Ceremony on Saturday, May 13. The ceremony will take place in Chosky Theater in CMU’s Purnell Center for the Arts and will feature remarks from the MFA class of 2023 and from undergraduate speakers Ameer Jones, Ester Petukhova, and Ajunie Virk. The public is also invited to join us for a reception immediately following the ceremony at the Miller ICA, where the Senior Exhibition is on view.
