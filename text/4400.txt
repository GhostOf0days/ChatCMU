The Office of the Vice Provost for Enrollment Management and Dean of Admission provides vision and leadership for all aspects of Carnegie Mellon’s enrollment process. From the point of recruitment, the office is committed to fostering an environment where students and families feel welcome, prepared and supported during their transition to life at CMU all the way through Commencement.
