Weekly Alumni News Update 11.27-12.04.18
Posted on November 26, 2018Welcome to the Weekly Alumni News Update! To have your news featured, please email schoolofartnews@cmu.edu with a brief description, link to more information, image, and your degree and class year. Or, add us to your newsletter list.
Patricia Hutton Gallery now represents Daniel Anthonisen BFA ’94 and will show four of his works in the Holiday Open House Show, opening Dec 1 in Doylestown, PA.
Jay Brooks BFA ’88 exhibits five paintings at the Salmagundi Club Thumb-Box show, opening Dec 3 in NYC. (image 3)
Lelia Byron BFA ‘13 received a grant from the Elizabeth Greenshields Foundation to pursue a MA Fine Art degree from Chelsea College of Arts in London. (image 1)
Janet Culbertson BFA ’53 exhibits in “Silver Linings” at Long Island Museum in Stony Brook, NY, opening Nov 30.
Jamie Earnest BFA ’16 exhibits in “Making Moves” at Goldmine Gallery in Pittsburgh, opening Nov 30.
Carolina Loyola-Garcia MFA ’00 is giving a workshop on bringing an idea to fruition without losing your sanity or going bankrupt at the Greater Pittsburgh Arts Council on Dec 3.
Patrick Meagher’s BFA ’95 book Andy Warhol in 15 Minutes is now available for pre-order. (image 4)
Marlena Novak BFA ‘79 received the Marion Kryczka Excellence in Teaching Award at the School of the Art Institute of Chicago. (image 2)
Yaeji (Kathy Lee) BHA ’15 was selected by Forbes for their “30 Under 30” topping charts and shifting culture in music.
