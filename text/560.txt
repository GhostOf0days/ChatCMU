 The Fence Post
The Fence Post is a monthly newsletter for family members of undergraduate students that provides brief updates and curated content about the CMU experience. Each issue will feature campus resources relevant to family members and upcoming campus events and deadlines.
Parents and family members are automatically subscribed to The Fence Post. If you have changed your contact information or would like to add an additional family member as a recipient, please email us.
