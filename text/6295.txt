Alumni News Update 11.02-11.08.21
Posted on November 2, 2021
Welcome to the weekly Alumni News Update! To have your news featured, please email schoolofartnews@cmu.edu with a brief description, link to more information, image, and your degree and class year. Or, add us to your newsletter list.
Kevin Brophy MFA ’17 had a solo exhibition titled “disappointing son of rich ppl” at Quaid Gallery in Tampa, FL, Sept 17 – Oct 1. 
Peter Burr’s BFA ’02 public artwork “The Continuous Movement” is on view at 150 N Riverside Plaza in Chicago, through Jan 31, 2022. 
Felipe Castelblanco MFA ’13 exhibits in “Where? Artistic Interventions” at Helmhaus Zürich, through Nov 14. 
Guild Hall Museum of East Hampton acquired Janet Culbertson’s BFA ‘53 oil painting of The First Billboard and its companion piece that depicts how the wetlands used to be and look.
Steve Gurysh MFA ’13 has a solo exhibition titled “Blue sky thunder days” at Wake Forest University, through Nov 13. 
PPOW presents a solo booth by Katharine Kuharic BFA ’84 for the ADAA: The Art Show in New York, Nov 3-7. 
Laurie Lipton BFA ’75 has a solo exhibition of drawings at California State University, Los Angeles’ Ronald H. Silverman Fine Arts Gallery. 
Claire McConaughy BFA ’81 has a solo exhibition titled “Nearby” at 490 Atlantic Gallery in Brookly, opening Nov 6. 
Shana Moulton MFA ’04 had a solo exhibition titled “All I need is the air I breathe” at Crèvecoeur Gallery in Paris, Sept 9 – Oct 14. 
Renée Stout BFA ’80 has a solo exhibition at Marc Straus Gallery in NYC, through Nov 13. 
Rebecca Vaughan MFA ’01 exhibits in “Uncanny Times: Looking Back, Looking Forward” on view at Seidel City in Boulder, CO through Nov 13.
