Professor Johannes DeYoung Presents work in Ars Electronica: Expanded Animation 2023
Posted on August 31, 2023
Professor Johannes DeYoung presents “The Endless Mile (Another Line of Becoming)” in the Expanded Animation program at this year’s Ars Electronica Festival in Linz, Austria. The event takes place at Ars Electronica Center between September 6 and 10, 2023.
Expanded Animation is part of the 2023 Ars Electronica Animation Festival, a diverse showcase that invites spectators to discover current artistic productions in the field of digital animation. The selection has been mostly compiled from the submissions at Prix Ars Electronica 2023, which shifted its focus this year from Computer Animation to the landscape of New Animation Art. It welcomes artists whose work expands on cutting-edge intersections of animation, art, and technology, delving into visual expression with unabashed experimentation. Part of a broader schema of New Animation Art, the eleventh edition of the Expanded Animation Symposium titled The Art of Performance, organized by the Hagenberg Campus of the University of Applied Sciences Upper Austria and Ars Electronica, aims to explore and discuss the various interfaces between animation, performance, and artistic expression from multiple viewpoints.
Extended Animation program website
