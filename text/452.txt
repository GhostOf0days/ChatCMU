Academic Advisory Center Dietrich College of Humanities and Social Sciences




 


 Welcome to the Academic Advisory Center (AAC), home to students from the Dietrich College of Humanities and Social Sciences.
After admission to Dietrich College, each undergraduate student will be assigned an AAC advisor who will provide information, advice and counseling about how to schedule classes, meet the requirements of the General Education Program (GenEd) and choose a degree to pursue from the various majors and minors available at Dietrich College.
The AAC is there for its students every step of the way and believes in its vision to be the nationally recognized role model of advising excellence.
