Give Kids the World is a non-profit organization in Kissimmee, Florida that provides week-long, all expenses paid vacations to children with life-threatening illnesses, along with their families. Give Kids provides not only week-long housing in a 84-acre, fully themed, spacious environment, but also events, meals, and activities. 180 staff and over 1,800 volunteers keep this Village running each and every week, providing children and their families with the best experience possible. To learn more about Give Kids the World, please visit www.gktw.orgHomeOverview
