Alumni News Update 02.21-02.27.23
Posted on February 20, 2023
Welcome to the weekly Alumni News Update! To have your news featured, please email schoolofartnews@cmu.edu with a brief description, link to more information, image, and your degree and class year. Or, add us to your newsletter list.
Reese Adams-Romagnoli BFA ’15 exhibits in “Muni Raise Me” at SOMARTS Cultural Center in San Francisco, through Apr 9. 
Kevin Brophy MFA ‘17 has a collaborative project with Rosa Nussbaum titled “Housewives of the Queer Hearth” on view at Charlotte Street Foundation in Kansas City, MO through Mar 4. Additional programming includes Anna Azizzy BFA ‘17 and Summer Jade Leavitt BFA ‘18. 
Ella Hepner BHA ’18 exhibits in “Nocturnes” at 345 Gallery in New York, through Mar 19. 
Cassandra C. Jones MFA ’04 showed work with Abigail Ogilvy Gallery at Spring/Break Art Show in Culver City, CA, Feb 15-19. 
Laura Karetzky BFA ‘87 has a video studio visit featured on the online outlet Gorky’s Granddaughter. 
Krisztina Lazar BFA ’04 show work in “Sky” at The Drawing Room in San Francisco, CA. 
Dana Lok BFA ’11 showed work with Miguel Abreu Gallery at Frieze LA, Feb 16-19. 
Carolyn Muskat BFA ’87 has a solo exhibition titled “Moving Forward, Looking Back” at NESTO Gallery Arts and Media Center at Milton Academy in Milton, AM, opening Feb 23.
Geraldine Ondrizek BFA ’85 exhibits in “Tenuous Threads” at the Atlantic Gallery in New York City, through Mar 4. 
Sung Rok Choi MFA ’12 has accepted an Assistant Professor position at Yonsei University Mirea Campus in Wonju City, South Korea.
