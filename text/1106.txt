University Communications & Marketing





 Capturing excellence, conveying impact
The Division of University Communications and Marketing leads and coordinates strategic marketing and communications for Carnegie Mellon University. We cultivate the CMU Brand and tell the university’s stories with a rich and authentic voice.
The division facilitates clear communication within the university and region. We raise the institution’s global visibility and deepen its reputation as one of the world’s great universities. And, we provide guidance and coordination for university leaders and communicators.
Together, we provide expertise in websites, design, videos, photographs, news packages, media relations, social media, and executive communications.
