Upcoming Events
SCS4All Bi-Weekly Meetings 

Our Mission
The SCS4ALL organization is a student-run initiative that works to develop a program of social and professional activities and leadership opportunities to broaden interest and participation in computing.

Get Involved
Join our cause: help us reach out to the world and spread the word that computer science is for all!
All SCS students are invited to join our biweekly meetings on Mondays at 6:00 PM Eastern Time on Zoom, starting on February 8, 2021.
