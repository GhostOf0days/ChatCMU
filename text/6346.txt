Weekly Alumni News Update 12.04-12.10.18
Posted on November 30, 2018Welcome to the Weekly Alumni News Update! To have your news featured, please email schoolofartnews@cmu.edu with a brief description, link to more information, image, and your degree and class year. Or, add us to your newsletter list.
Milo Berezin BFA ’08, Paul Peng BCSA ’17, and Mary Tremonte BHA ’00 participate in Assemble’s HyperLocal Queer Craft Market on Dec 9.
Mel Bochner BFA ’62 exhibits with Peter Freeman Gallery at Art Basel Miami Beach, Dec 5-9.
Hannah Epstein MFA ’17 shows work in a group booth with Steve Turner Gallery at UNTITLED Miami Beach, Dec 5-9.
Jenna Houston BHA ‘18 has a solo show titled “If I had hair, I’d want you to pull it” at Late Space in Pittsburgh, opening Dec 7.
Maria Mangano BFA ’06 and Sarah Marshall BFA ‘92 exhibit in “Printwork 2018” at Artists Image Resource, opening Dec 7.
Teresa Westkaemper BFA ’84 exhibits in “Craft Forms 2018” at the Wayne Art Center in Wayne, PA, opening Dec 8.
Seldon Yuan BHA ’98 and Janine Biunno BFA ’04 exhibit in “The 2019 Flat File: Year Six” at Tiger Strikes Asteroid in Brooklyn through Dec 16.
