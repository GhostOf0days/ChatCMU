The Project


Morality Play
Morality Play is the joint transmedia venture being undertaken by both a team at the Entertainment Technology Center and students in Carnegie Mellon University’s Philosophy Department. Interweaving a plethora of media, such as digital video, social media websites, and games, the project will produce an engaging experience designed to show people the facts about economic inequality in America. What caused it? What is fair? What can be done? These are questions that will be touched upon from as many viewpoints as possible.
