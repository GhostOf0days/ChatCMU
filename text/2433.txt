Projects that Inspired the Creative Good Fund
The ETC has done numerous projects with Give Kids the world over the past decade that inspired the Creative Good Fund. Through our work, we have created thousands of memories for children with life threatening illnesses and their families.
Past ETC projects with Give Kids the World include:
StargazerMiracle WorkshopMiracle Workshop 2.0The Big SurpriseGold StarsKissimee InteractiveSweet DreamsStardustStarscape
Carnegie Mellon Today also has a good article on the ETC’s history with Give Kids the World.
