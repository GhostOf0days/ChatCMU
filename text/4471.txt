MCS Technology Services is the collaboration of MCS IT and MCS Communications staff. We offer a variety of technology services to the MCS community including End-Point Computing Support, Research Computing Support, Web Services, as well as provide Technical Consulting, Technical Procurement and we collaborate closely with central campus technology groups such as Computing Services, Research Computing Advisory Council, and the Academic Computing Group.
