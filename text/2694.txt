MSCV Program Tuition

Tuition for the 2023-24 academic year is $54,072 + mandatory fees. Fee amounts are published here by the University. Students are not assessed tuition for a summer internship.
Students hoping to take courses beyond those required for MSCV must obtain written permission from the MSCV Program Director. If approved, the student will be charged additional tuition at a rate of $751 per unit.
Students should contact The HUB to learn more about  Graduate Student Financial Aid or to submit billing/payment questions.
