SEI Zero Trust Industry Days 2023
August 2023 •  Fact Sheet 
The SEI is hosting Zero Trust Industry Days 2023 to collect information from those who develop solutions for implementing a zero trust architecture.




Publisher:
Software Engineering Institute











Subjects


Enterprise Risk and Resilience ManagementEnterprise Risk and Resilience Management







Abstract

The Software Engineering Institute (SEI) is hosting Zero Trust Industry Days 2023 to collect information from those who develop solutions for implementing a zero trust architecture. Presenters will contribute their ideas, solutions, and experiences to help organizations form a zero trust implementation that meets their mission goals, budget, and time frame.
This event is a request for information (RFI) exercise, held in a hybrid environment at the SEI in October 2023. It will focus on how organizations can comply with the following guidance documents:

M-22-09—Moving the U.S. Government Toward Zero Trust Cybersecurity Principles 
M-21-31—Improving the Federal Government’s Investigative and Remediation Capabilities Related to Cybersecurity Incidents 
CISA Zero Trust Maturity Model, Version 2.0 
National Cybersecurity Strategy
DoD Zero Trust Strategy

Abstract

The Software Engineering Institute (SEI) is hosting Zero Trust Industry Days 2023 to collect information from those who develop solutions for implementing a zero trust architecture. Presenters will contribute their ideas, solutions, and experiences to help organizations form a zero trust implementation that meets their mission goals, budget, and time frame.

M-21-31—Improving the Federal Government’s Investigative and Remediation Capabilities Related to Cybersecurity Incidents 
CISA Zero Trust Maturity Model, Version 2.0
