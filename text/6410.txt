Moving Image Studio Presents ‘Am I Real Yet?’ on May 14
Posted on May 10, 2021
Join us on May 14 at 8:00 PM ET for “Am I Real Yet?,” a livestream screening of new moving image work created by students in Carnegie Mellon University School of Art’s Moving Image Studio. Our screening will livestream on Twitch. At 8:45 PM ET, the artists will host a Q & A conversation on Zoom.
This marvelous line-up includes animation, music video, documentary, comedy, gender euphoria and much, much more by: Najjiyya Al-Mahi, Michelle Cao, Jean-Luc DeLadurantaye, Uni Hunte, Brandon Hyun, Grace Kang, Steven Montinar, TSM, Khushi Nandgaonkar, Vivian Noh, Ava Schieferstein.
May 14, 20218:00pm (ET) – “Am I Real Yet?” on the MovingImageStudio Twitch Channel8:45pm (ET) – Q & A with the Artists on Zoom
Moving Image Studio Instructor: Suzie Silver
