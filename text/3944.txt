Task Force on the CMU Experience



 


 Task Force on the CMU Experience
On November 20, 2019, President Farnam Jahanian announced in a campus-wide email that the Task Force on the CMU Experience will transition its initiatives to become university commitments at all levels of our organization.
In 2016, CMU leadership convened an internal campus-wide task force to closely examine the CMU experience. A total of 30 faculty members, staff, students, alumni, and senior administrators served as members of the Task Force, with an additional 200+ members of the campus community engaged in initiative teams related to health and wellness, academic policies, and faculty and staff development over the course of three years.
Read Provost Garrett's letter to the university
