Alumni News Update 02.28-03.06.23
Posted on February 27, 2023
Welcome to the weekly Alumni News Update! To have your news featured, please email schoolofartnews@cmu.edu with a brief description, link to more information, image, and your degree and class year. Or, add us to your newsletter list.
Adria Arch BFA ‘74 has a solo exhibition titled “Sirens and Sentinels” at Boston Sculptors Gallery, opening Mar 1. 
Joy Poulard Cruz MFA ’16-‘19 exhibits in “Fifty: An Alumni Exhibition” at the Museum of Contemporary Art Jacksonville, opening Mar 25. 
Petra Floyd MFA ‘22 has a solo exhibition titled “Before the DJ was Sound, and She Played It” at Fjord in Philadelphia, PA, opening Mar 11. 
Mary Maggic BSA ‘13 exhibits in “Kingdom of the Ill” at the Museum of Contemporary Art in Bozen-Bolzano, Italy, through Mar 5. 
Alicia Ott BFA ‘17 has a solo exhibition titled “memory meditations” at Pullproof Studio in Pittsburgh on Mar 3. 
Abigail Satinsky BFA ‘03, Tufts University Art Gallery Curator & Head of Public Engagement, has been appointed as the Program Officer & Curator, Arts and Culture, at the Wagner Foundation, based in Cambridge, MA.⁠
Laurie Shapiro’s BFA ‘12 installation “We Are All Connected to Each Other Through Nature” will be on view at the San Diego Museum of Art from Mar 31 to Apr 2. 
Dave Vranich BFA ‘96 exhibits in the “61st Long Island Artists Exhibition” at the Art League of Long Island, opening Mar 4.
