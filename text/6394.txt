Announcing the 2022 Art Award Winners!
Posted on April 18, 2022
The School of Art congratulates the winners of our annual art awards! See more work by this year’s award winners in our online exhibition! You can also see the work in person in the Ellis Gallery from April 20 – 24.

First Year AwardsDariyah Scott: Anne Ophelia Dowden AwardHou Shanting: Anne Ophelia Dowden AwardMargaret Tambke: Wilfred Readio AwardLillian Vanveen: Wilfred Readio Award
Sophomore AwardsPeggy Shen: Anne Ophelia Dowden AwardKatie Frazee: Samuel Rosenberg AwardStacey Cho: J. B Ellis AwardNatsume Takumi: A.F Porter Award
Junior AwardsAjunie Virk: Samuel Rosenberg AwardMilo Tayao: Samuel Rosenberg AwardEster Petukhova: E.P. Howard AwardAngelica Bonilla: Marjory Glassburn Francis Award
Senior AwardsBeatriz Echeverria: John L. Porter AwardJonas Petkus: Samuel Rosenberg AwardKhushi Nandgaonkar: Samuel Rosenberg AwardAshton Jones: Samuel Rosenberg AwardLily Bridges: Marjory Glassburn Francis Award
Dara Birnbaum AwardVivian NohInterdisciplinary AwardLena ChenC.G. Douglas “Wrong-Way” Corrigan Travel FellowshipAnisha Baid
Maddy Varner Mastication Grant for Time and/or Teeth Based WorksAwarded by the Frank-Ratchye STUDIO for Creative InquiryBenford Krummenacher
