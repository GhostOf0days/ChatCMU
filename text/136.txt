 Student Organization Resources
SLICE provides support and resources to the 400+ student-run organizations recognized by Student Government and the more than 20 sororities and fraternities active on Carnegie Mellon's campus.
Student organization leaders and members work together to create and engage in meaningful and enriching experiences through their organizations and events. They contribute to the CMU community and beyond through their individual missions and goals, creating the rich and diverse fabric of student engagement and involvement at CMU.
