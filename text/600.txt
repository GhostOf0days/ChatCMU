George N. Pauly, Jr. Professorship in Architecture





Carnegie Mellon Architecture’s Visiting Professorships welcome early to mid-career practitioners, scholars and studio educators with focused research and/or creative work interested in teaching.We offer two-year visiting professorship appointments to promote deeper immersion in the school community. The call for applications for the 2024-26 Joseph F. Thomas Visiting Professorship will be announced in the fall of 2023.
